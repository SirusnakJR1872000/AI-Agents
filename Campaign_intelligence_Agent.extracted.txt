CAMPAIGN INTELLIGENCE – AS IS
Siloed Data Extraction: Compiling basic delivery metrics requires highly labor-intensive manual extraction from multiple disconnected systems.
Guesswork in Trend Analysis: Spotting performance trends involves manually scanning historical data, which wastes hours and relies on subjective guesswork and meetings to determine the underlying causes.
Disjointed Channel Comparisons: Comparing performance across Email, SMS, WhatsApp, and Push demands tedious cross-referencing between disjointed platforms.
Painstaking Creative Tracking: Tracking engagement for specific creatives, variants, and CTAs requires manual mapping and logging across every channel.
